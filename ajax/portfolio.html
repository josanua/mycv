industria.be - Site of the student association of the Faculty of Industrial Engineering KU Leuven Campus Group T.

On this site you will find, among other things, the Kursusdienst (course book service) platform and more information about our association. E-shop of courses and students needs.

Working period: September 2021 - April 2022

Languages/Technologies:

  • Wordpress Multisite Installation, WooCommerce, Bedrock and Sage boilerplates, Composer, WP-CLI
  • PHP, Composer, was implemented Laravel Framework custom code architecture,
  • HTML, Laravel Blade templating, SCSS, Vanilla JS, jQuery, ReactJS components, webpack builder
  • Docker (docker-compose), Nginx

Role: Back and Front-end developer

No. of developers: 1

Responsibilities:

  • Take the project for maintenance and further features development
  • Involved and participated in daily scrums and monthly sprint planning
  • Create custom PHP code, Front-end JS and Blade Templating, customising Gutenberg WP blocks, custom coding of ReactJS interfaces.
  • Did debug of existing issues
  • Do documentation
